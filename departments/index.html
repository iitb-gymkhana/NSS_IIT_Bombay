Educational Outreach

Children are the future of our country and illiteracy is the biggest thing that darkens it. Dispelling the darkness that has prevailed for too long and doing our bit in educating the future of the nation, this stands as the aim for Educational Outreach. We along with some NGOs make an effort in spreading the light of knowledge among the people who are out of its reach. The students of IIT Bombay voluntarily teach the under privileged children. We also understand that formal education can't make a person complete and extra-curricular activities play a major role in shaping one's personality. And hence, for the all-round development of children we impart computer education and conduct hobby classes for those kids who don't have access to such opportunities. Finally ending up leaving happiness filled hearts to the volunteers for getting a real value to their education through the joys of children. Yes...We are special, because we choose to serve the society by educating it.
