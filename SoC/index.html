Open Learning Initiative

The naxal-affected Giridih district in Jharkhand has started an e-learning initiative (Disha Giridih) for its students to educate them in the absence of Government teachers and that videos on our YouTube channel, OLI were helping them provide regional language content to the students. On interaction with them, the Web-learning Coordinator of Giridih had the following to say: "Most of the students belong to rural areas and govt Hindi medium schools. Videos from OLI NSS are totally apt for them as the content is presented in Hindi in verbal as well as in written format. Also,the content is presented in a very simple manner and it helps our students get the basic concepts....students can ask questions to their teachers and due to local language they can even discuss issues among their friends...As per our monthly assessment report, there is a huge demand of new videos from students as well as teachers. Students are very much excited because of the e-learning initiative and their attendance has increased due to such initiatives."
