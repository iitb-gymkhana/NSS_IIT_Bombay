Vinayak Gupta

Often, it is not until your adult life that you go through some of your most valuable events. For instance, some may find their time in the military to be of utmost importance to them, while others sometimes find that their experience as a parent was the most rewarding one they’ve had. Personally though, I consider my time in green campus as my most valuable experience yet. On the first Day, completely drenched in rain ,all the Members of the NSS, GC reached Campus Hub for a “Sapling Collection Drive”.Apart from being a fun activity, I met some of my best friends in the institute right here in Green Campus. My most memorable experience was the visit to Sameer Hills. We took water and manure with us and climbed all the way up. It was very tiring but so much fun as we all were together. The AAs kept motivating us and this led us all the way up to the top. We dug a hole and planted a tree in it.
